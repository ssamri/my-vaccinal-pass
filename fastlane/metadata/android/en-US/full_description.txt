With the application my vaccine pass, it is possible to keep your own certificate. This makes the app the ideal solution to have your vaccination pass close at hand!

you just need to import your vaccine certificate in PDF format, as well as that of your family, and show them to law enforcement for all your trips and trips.

the application my vaccine pass is in the form of a document holder (wallet), an application where you can store the certificates to have them at hand at any time if necessary.

Please note that no internet connection is required, all your documents are stored on your mobile phone app only.
